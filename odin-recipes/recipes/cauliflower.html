Cauliflower Recipe

Description - how to make!

Have a bite at this 100% vegetarian meal that replaces chicken for cauliflower and chickpeas! I was truly sceptical the first time I threw this one into the pan, but it has turned out to become a household favorite - at least for me ;)

Ingredients

  • One large cauliflower
  • Roughly 200g chickpeas
  • One ready to cook indian chicken tika pack (maybe only available in Norway ¯\_(ツ)_/¯)
  • 1.5dl creme fraiche
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Cut the cauliflower into appropriately small pieces (should be easy to eat)
  2. Drench the chickpeas
  3. Add the chickpeas, cauliflower and some oil into a pan
  4. Add in the "marinade" bag and mix thoroughly
  5. Let sit for 10 minutes to marinade
  6. Meanwhile, cook the rice that came with the ready-to-cook pack
  7. Start frying the chickpeas and cauliflower on a medium-high heat
  8. Meanwhile, add 3dl of water, 1.5dl of creme fraiche and the "saus" bag that came with the ready-to-cook pack into a pot
  9. Mix thoroughly and heat up until boiling. Let boil for 4 minutes, stir throughout
  10. Add the newly cooked sauce into the properly fried pan of chickpeas and cauliflower
  11. Let cook for another few minutes
  12. Serve the rice onto a plate alongside the chickpeas and cauliflower and voila, enjoy!
