Salmon Recipe

Description - how to make!

Enjoy this crispy, tasty and very nutritious plate of salmon, veggies and rice! This one's a favorite between me and my girlfriend and I assure you that if you fry the salmon correctly you'll never turn back to your other meals ever again.

Ingredients

  • 250g salmon
  • 200g frozen vegetables
  • 50g frozen spinach
  • 75g basmati rice
  • Lemon-pepper
  • Curry powder
  • Paprika powder
  • Dried basil
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Add the salmon to the pan and drissle the pan with oil
  2. Add lemon-pepper in moderate amounts onto the salmon
  3. Fry the salmon on medium-high heat until crispy and cooked throughout
  4. Meanwhile, fry the frozen vegetables and add a few cubes of frozen spinach to the mix
  5. Season with curry and paprika powder, and sprinkle with some basil throughout
  6. Cook the rice while the other spans are frying
  7. Once all these 3 parts of the dish are done, serve on a plate and enjoy!
